Penguins

Introduction

Penguins (order Sphenisciformes, family Spheniscidae) are a group of aquatic flightless birds.

Pinguinus

Since 1871, the Latin word Pinguinus has been used in scientific classification to name the genus of the great auk (Pinguinus impennis, meaning "penguin without flight feathers"), which became extinct in the mid-19th century.
